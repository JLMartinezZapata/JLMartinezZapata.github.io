Factoring Trinomial using AC Method


Solve: 6x³-40x²+56x


  • Step1: 2x [3x²-20x+28]

    Factor out anything common to all terms.


  • Step2: 2x [3x²+(20)x+28]

    Write trinomial in standard form ax²+bx+c

  • Step3: 2x [3x²+(20)x+(+28)]

    Determine product of a*c=3*28=84

  • Step4:

    List all pairs of factors of a*c. If a*c is negative , then factors have opposite signs.
    If a*c is positve, then factors have same signs. Sign of b determines sign of factors.
    Factors of 84 are:

    -1,-84 -4,-21 -6,-14 -7,-12


    Select factor pair such that their sum is b term = -20

  • Step5:

    Split middle term b order factors as multiple of the a and c

    2x[3x²+(-6)x+(-14)x+(+28)]


  • Step6:

    Factor out something common to first two terms.

    2x[3x²+(-6)x+(-14)x+(+28)→2x[3x(x-2)+(-14)x+(+28)]


  • Step7:

    Factor out same binomial in last two terms.

  • Step8:

    Apply Distributive Law and convert trinomial into the product of two binomials and a monomial.
